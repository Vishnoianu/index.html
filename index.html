Privacy Policy — Glass Widgets Ultra AI

Last updated: 6 July 2026

Glass Widgets Ultra AI ("our app", "we", "us") is an Android widget application. This Privacy Policy clearly explains what information the app collects, why it collects it, and where that information is saved and used.

Key Points at a Glance

Does the app collect user data?Yes — only approximate location and step count (both device-level, explained below)
Why is location used?Only in the Weather widget to show your city's weather
Is data saved anywhere?Yes, but only on your own phone (local storage). Nothing is saved on our servers
Are any third-party APIs used?Yes — OpenWeatherMap (for weather data) and Google AdMob (for ads)

1. What Information We Collect and Why

(a) Location (approximate, city-level):
The Weather widget uses your device's approximate location to show nearby weather. This only happens when you use the Weather widget. This location is sent directly to OpenWeatherMap (a third-party weather service) to get temperature and city data — this data never reaches our servers because we don't have one.

(b) Physical Activity / Steps:
The Steps widget reads only a number (total steps) from your device's built-in step-counter sensor so the widget can display your steps. This data is never sent anywhere — not to us, not to anyone else.

(c) Notes, Widget Styles, Water-Tracker Count:
Any notes you write, widget styles you choose, or water-tracker counts you record — all are saved only in your phone's local storage (SharedPreferences). Nothing is sent over the internet.

(d) Camera (Flashlight widget):
The Flashlight widget only accesses the camera's "torch mode" to turn the LED light on/off. No photos, videos, or camera feed are accessed or saved.

2. Advertising & Google AdMob

This app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. When ads are displayed, Google and its partners may collect data about your device and how you interact with ads. This includes:

  • Device identifiers and information
  • IP address
  • Ad performance data
  • Analytics about ad interactions

This data collection is controlled by Google's own Privacy Policy, which you can find here: Google Privacy Policy

You can also opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings: Settings → Google → Manage Your Google Account → Data & Privacy → Ad Settings.

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • On your own phone (locally): Notes, widget styles, water-tracker count
  • Not stored anywhere, only used temporarily: Location (for Weather API call), Step count (to display in widget)
  • On Google's servers (AdMob): Ad-related data for analytics and ad delivery
  • On our servers: Nothing — this app has no backend server

4. Third-Party Services

Two third-party services are used:

  • OpenWeatherMap: Provides weather data for the Weather widget. Their Privacy Policy: openweathermap.org/privacy-policy
  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements. Their Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell or share any of your personal information with third parties (except as described above for ad delivery), nor do we directly share it with advertising companies other than Google AdMob.

6. Permissions the App Requests

  • Location (Coarse): For the Weather widget — without it, the widget shows "Location Permission Needed"
  • Physical Activity: For the Steps widget (only required on Android 10+)
  • Internet: To fetch weather data and display ads
  • Camera (Torch mode only): For the Flashlight widget

All permissions are optional — if you don't grant a permission, only that specific widget or feature won't work; the rest of the app functions normally.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not specifically designed for children under 13 years old, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we add new features or third-party services that change how data is handled, this policy will be updated with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please email: vishnoia255@gmail.com
